My Top 5 Tourist Attractions

Gardens by the Bay

Gardens by the Bay is a futuristic vision of artificial landscapes situated in Singapore. It's eco friendly design incorporates smart self-sustaining technologies, such as it's self watering mechanism which collects rain water.

Tower Bridge

An icon of London, it's timeless design and it's creation at the time of the start of the industrial era, makes it a great visit. You can walk along the skybridge, getting a higher view of London and look down from it's glass floor.

Great Wall of China

More than 20 times the length of the UK, the great wall of china is an iconic landmark.

Pyramids of Giza

An iconic landmark, the pyramids of Giza were constructred over 4000 years ago. The construction of it is a fascinating mystery, as each block weighed several tons.

The Colosseum

Nearly 2000 years old, the colosseum is a staple of roman success and culture. A ground for entertainment through fighting, it displays how a common sport throughout history is one painted by violence.
